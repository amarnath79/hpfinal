Cisco Certified Network Associate

CCNA is a popular internship Program among computer network engineers. It is estimated that more than 1 million CCNA certificates have been awarded since it was first launched in 1998. The CCNA certificate covers a broad range of networking concepts. It helps candidates to prepare for the latest network technologies they are likely to work on.


  1. 1.Develop a working knowledge of routing, switching, network applications, protocols, and services.

  2. 2.Study with an instructor in the classroom and access expert content online anytime.

  3. 3.Practice what you learn on both real equipment and Cisco Packet Tracer, a network configuration simulation tool.

  4. 4.Get immediate feedback on your work through built-in quizzes and tests.

  5. 5.Prepare for the workplace with collaborative projects and presentations.

  6. 6.Connect with the global Cisco Networking Academy community.
